Sanjeevani – Complaint Support & Help Guide

The Sanjeevani platform provides a simple and transparent system for raising and tracking complaints related to agricultural schemes, herb traceability, platform issues, and incentives. Any farmer, consumer, or stakeholder can raise a complaint directly from the portal.

What is the “Raise a Complaint” Feature?

The "Raise a Complaint" button on the home page allows users to report issues they face while using the Sanjeevani platform. Once submitted, the system generates a unique Complaint ID. This ID helps users track the progress and resolution of their issue.

Who Can Raise a Complaint?

Farmers

Women Farmers

Consumers

Ayurvedic Companies

Government Officials

Any user facing portal-related issues

Common Issues You Can Report

Farmers may raise complaints about:

Payment delays or missing incentives

Incorrect eligibility status for schemes

Wrong batch mapping

QR code generation issues

Errors in lab report visibility

Trouble logging in or registering

Problems with uploading documents

Incorrect personal or farm details not updating

Consumers may complain about:

QR code not scanning

Missing or incorrect herb journey information

Authenticity concerns

Incomplete batch details

Companies may complain about:

Incorrect supplier batch data

Missing certifications

Platform accessibility issues

Other general complaints:

Website not loading

Server issues

Unexpected errors

Information not updating

How to Raise a Complaint (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Click “Raise a Complaint” on the homepage.

Step 2: Select your user type:

Farmer

Consumer

Company

Government / Admin

Step 3: Fill in the details:

Name

Contact number or email

Complaint category

Description of the issue

Batch ID (if applicable)

Upload supporting documents (optional)

Step 4: Submit the complaint.

Step 5: Receive a system-generated Complaint ID.
This ID can be used to track your complaint status.

Tracking Your Complaint

After submitting a complaint, users can track updates using:

Complaint Tracking portal

SMS notifications (if enabled)

Email alerts (if provided)

Complaint statuses include:

Submitted

Under Review

Forwarded to Department

Action in Progress

Resolved

Closed

Expected Resolution Time

Most complaints are resolved within:

24–72 hours for platform-related issues

3–7 days for administrative or incentive issues

7–14 days for batch, QR, and laboratory-related disputes

Important Guidelines for Submitting Complaints

Provide accurate batch ID (if applicable).

Add clear details to help faster resolution.

Attach screenshots or proof for complex issues.

Use the same phone number or email for tracking.

Do not submit duplicate complaints for the same issue.

Support Channels

Users can also contact Sanjeevani support through:

Portal helpdesk

Helpline number (if provided by gov. department)

Email support team

Local agricultural officer or field coordinator

Why Complaint Support Matters

The goal of Sanjeevani’s complaint system is to:

Ensure transparency

Resolve farmer and consumer issues quickly

Improve trust in the traceability platform

Provide accountability across the herbal value chain

Support fair practices and improve user experience
