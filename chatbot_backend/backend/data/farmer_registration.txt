Sanjeevani Farmer Registration Help Guide
The Sanjeevani platform enables farmers to register themselves, access government-backed herbal cultivation schemes, receive incentives, and participate in the blockchain-based traceability system. Registration is designed to be simple, transparent, and accessible for all farmers, including women farmers and small-scale cultivators.
Who Can Register on Sanjeevani?
Farmers who meet any of the following criteria can register:

• Individuals cultivating Ayurvedic or medicinal herbs
• Members of Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs)
• Women farmers leading or managing herbal cultivation
• Herbal cluster farmers
• Organic or sustainable farming practitioners
• Farmers participating in government-supported herbal programs

Why Should Farmers Register?

Registration allows farmers to:

• Access government schemes and incentives
• Receive higher payments for certified, quality herbs
• Get unique batch IDs for herb traceability
• Create a digital identity for market visibility
• Participate in QR-based supply chain verification
• Access training and support programs
• Track lab test reports and batch status online

How to Register on Sanjeevani (Step-by-Step)
Step 1: Visit the Sanjeevani portal homepage.
Step 2: Click the Register as Farmer button.
Step 3: Enter your basic details:

Full Name
Phone Number
Address
Aadhaar Number
Gender (special benefits for women farmers)

After submission:
✔ Your farmer profile is created
✔ You receive a confirmation SMS or email
✔ You can now log in to access your dashboard

Farmer Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, farmers can:

• View their registered farm details
• Register new herb batches
• Download QR codes for traceability
• Track lab testing status
• View accepted/rejected batch history
• Check incentives and payments
• Access training materials and support
• Raise complaints if needed
