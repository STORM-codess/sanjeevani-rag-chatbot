What is Sanjeevani?

Sanjeevani is a government-backed system that makes Ayurvedic herbs transparent and trustworthy.
Consumers can scan QR codes on products to check where the herbs came from, how they were grown, and what quality tests they passed.

How does Sanjeevani help me as a consumer?

• You can verify if a product is genuine
• You can see where the herbs were grown
• Check lab test results for safety
• Ensure there is no adulteration
• Feel confident about what you are buying

How do I check if a product is authentic?

Look for a QR code on the product label.
Scan it using your mobile camera.

You will see:
• Herb source (farm details)
• Batch journey
• Test results
• Certification status

If information is missing → the product may not be genuine.

What does the QR code show?

The QR code provides:
• Farmer details (as permitted)
• Farm location
• Herb quality test reports
• Batch ID and history
• Processing and packaging steps

It is like the “story” of your product.

What if the QR code does not work?

If the QR code does not scan:
• Check your internet connection
• Try scanning in better light
• Restart your camera

If still not working → raise a complaint through the Sanjeevani platform.

How do I know if an Ayurvedic herb is good quality?

General signs of good quality herbs:
• Natural smell
• Clean and free from dust or fungus
• Not too old or discolored
• Dry but not brittle
• No artificial colors

Each herb has its own characteristics, but these rules apply to most.

Are Ayurvedic products safe to use?

Yes, if purchased from trusted brands that follow Sanjeevani traceability and testing.

Safety tips:
• Always check the QR code
• Read ingredients
• Follow recommended dosage
• Avoid expired products

Consult a doctor if you have medical conditions.

What if I find something suspicious in a product?

You can immediately raise a complaint on the Sanjeevani platform.

Consumers can report:
• Fake or duplicate products
• Wrong QR code information
• Mismatch in batch details
• Poor quality or contamination

A complaint ID will be given so you can track the action taken.

Can I trust the herb information shown in the QR code?

Yes.
The data is recorded using blockchain technology, which means once details are entered, they cannot be changed or faked.

This ensures authenticity for consumers.

Do I need to make an account to use Sanjeevani?

No.
You can scan QR codes and view product details without creating an account.

What type of Ayurvedic products support Sanjeevani traceability?

Products that have QR-based traceability, such as:
• Herbal powders
• Tablets and capsules
• Oils
• Churnas
• Ready-to-use herbal products

More product categories are being added gradually.

Can I check herb benefits using Sanjeevani?

Yes.
Scanning the QR code or asking the Sanjeevani chatbot can give information about:
• Herb uses
• Health benefits
• Safety
• How it is grown
• How to identify good quality

What should I do if the product information is missing?

You can:
• Try scanning again
• Contact customer support
• Raise a complaint with screenshots

Missing information may indicate a non-registered or unsafe product.

Can I compare different herb batches?

Yes.
Each batch has:
• Its own batch ID
• Quality score
• Farm details
• Harvest date

You can compare batches to choose the best quality.

Is Sanjeevani available for all Ayurvedic brands?

Not yet.
It is currently available only for participating manufacturers.
The list is growing as more companies join.

Does Sanjeevani store my personal details?

No.
Consumers do not need to provide any personal information to scan QR codes or check product authenticity.

What if I need help or cannot understand the information?

You can:
• Ask the Sanjeevani chatbot
• Read help articles on the platform
• Raise a complaint for support

The platform is designed to be simple and consumer-friendly.
