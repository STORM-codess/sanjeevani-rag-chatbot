Sanjeevani – Empowering Farmers, Protecting Ayurveda, Building Trust

Sanjeevani is a government initiative that ensures traceability and trust in Ayurvedic herbs through blockchain technology. It rewards farmers for quality produce, supports women farmers, and allows consumers to verify authenticity with QR codes, while promoting sustainable farming and preserving India’s Ayurvedic heritage.

Main Actions on Home Page

Register as Farmer: Join the Sanjeevani platform to access schemes, incentives, and training.

Sign In: Existing farmers, officials, and partners can log in to view dashboards, track batches, and manage submissions.

Raise a Complaint: Any farmer, consumer, or stakeholder can raise a complaint related to:

About Sanjeevani

Sanjeevani is a transparent and farmer-centric platform that connects the entire Ayurvedic herb value chain using blockchain-based traceability.

Key pillars of Sanjeevani:

Trust: Every herb batch can be verified from farm to product.

Fairness: Farmers are rewarded for clean, certified, and high-quality produce.

Inclusion: Women farmers receive dedicated support and recognition.

Sustainability: Encourages climate-resilient, chemical-free, and traditional farming practices.

Heritage: Protects and promotes India’s rich Ayurvedic legacy.

Schemes and Support for Farmers

Quality Incentive Scheme
Farmers who supply high-quality, lab-tested Ayurvedic herbs receive:

Extra price for certified batches

Priority listing for trusted buyers

Digital quality certificate visible to companies and consumers

Sustainable Cultivation Support Scheme
To promote eco-friendly farming, Sanjeevani supports farmers with:

Guidance on organic and sustainable cultivation practices

Support for soil health management

Incentives for following environment-friendly standards

Women Farmer Empowerment Scheme
Women farmers are given focused benefits:

Special incentives for registered women-led farms and FPOs

Priority access to training programs and workshops

Visibility and recognition on the platform as “Sanjeevani Women Champions”

Digital Traceability & QR Code Assistance
Farmers registered with Sanjeevani get:

Unique batch IDs mapped to their produce

QR codes that consumers can scan to see farm details, test results, and journey

More trust from companies and end consumers, leading to better market value

Farmer Training & Capacity Building
Sanjeevani regularly organizes:

Online and offline training on good agricultural practices (GAP)

Sessions on post-harvest handling, drying, storage, and grading of herbs

Awareness on certification, documentation, and quality standards

How Sanjeevani Benefits You

For Farmers
Earn more for high-quality, certified Ayurvedic herbs through a transparent system. Build your reputation as a trusted supplier with every successful batch and lab-certified result.

For Women Farmers
Access special incentives, training, and recognition for your contribution. Get support to form women-led groups, cooperatives, or FPOs and become visible leaders in the herbal value chain.

For Consumers
Trust in safe, authentic Ayurvedic products with verifiable origins. By scanning the QR code, consumers can see:

Farm location and farmer details (as permitted)

Herb journey from farm to processing

Quality test results and certifications

For Society
Support sustainable farming and help preserve India’s rich Ayurvedic heritage. Sanjeevani promotes responsible sourcing, fair systems, and awareness about genuine Ayurvedic herbs.

For Government
Ensure regulatory compliance and transparency across the supply chain. Sanjeevani helps in:

Monitoring herb sourcing and movement

Reducing fraud and adulteration

Supporting policy decisions with reliable data

Raise a Complaint – We’re Here to Help

If you face any issue related to:

Payment or incentive not received

Incorrect batch mapping or QR code problem

Wrong scheme eligibility or rejected application

Platform or login issues

You can click the “Raise a Complaint” button on the home page.

Steps:

Select your user type (Farmer / Consumer / Other)

Enter basic details and describe the issue

Attach optional proof (invoice, screenshot, batch ID)

Submit and receive a Complaint ID

You can later track your complaint status through the portal or support desk.
