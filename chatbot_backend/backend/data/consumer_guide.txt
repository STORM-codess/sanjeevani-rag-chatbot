Ashwagandha – For Stress Relief & Strength

What It Is:
A root used for reducing stress and improving energy.

Why Consumers Like It:

Helps with stress and anxiety

Improves sleep quality

Supports stamina and overall strength

Often used in supplements for mental calmness

How It Is Used:
Ashwagandha is usually taken as capsules, powder, or mixed with warm milk.

How to Identify Good Quality:

Roots should be clean and firm

Mild earthy smell

Light brown color

Where It Comes From in India:
Mostly from Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, and Gujarat.

Acts as a gentle detoxifier
